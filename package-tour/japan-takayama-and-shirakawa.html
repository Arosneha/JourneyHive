Itinerary

Discover the traditional villages of Takayama and Shirakawa-go, enjoy scenic landscapes, cultural heritage, and local markets over 7 days.

Day 1: Arrival in Takayama +
Arrive in Takayama, check-in to your hotel, and explore the Old Town area at leisure.
Day 2: Takayama Morning Markets & Cultural Sites +
Visit the morning markets, Takayama Jinya, and traditional streets filled with local shops.
Day 3: Shirakawa-go Village +
Day trip to Shirakawa-go UNESCO World Heritage site. Explore gassho-style houses and scenic surroundings.
Day 4: Hida Folk Village +
Visit Hida Folk Village to learn about traditional crafts and local culture.
Day 5: Scenic Villages & Countryside +
Enjoy a scenic drive and visit nearby villages to experience rural Japanese life.
Day 6: Free Day / Optional Tours +
Relax or take optional trips to nearby attractions like Takayama Festivals or local workshops.
Day 7: Departure +
Check out and transfer to airport or station for onward journey.
